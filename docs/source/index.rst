Welcome to qdynpylib documentation!
=====================================

qdynpylib_ provides a collection of utilities to augment the Fortran QDYN
library for quantum dynamics and control, developed in-house in the `Koch group`_
at the University of Kassel.

.. _qdynpylib: https://github.com/goerz/qdynpylib#qdynpylib
.. _`Koch group`: http://www.uni-kassel.de/fb10/en/institutes/physics/research-groups/quantum-dynamics-and-control/homepage.html
